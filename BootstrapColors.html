Demo for Bootstrap Colors Extended

(https://github.com/robert1233/BootstrapColorsExtended)

The following new colors are now added to bootstrap! Just use the tags like you normally would. For example: btn-grape, or bg-indigo-light!

bg-gray
bg-yellow
bg-lime
bg-teal
bg-navy
bg-indigo
bg-grape
bg-pink
bg-vermillion
bg-orange
bg-brown
bg-maroon
bg-steel
bg-green

The above colors also have light and dark versions for text color, background color, and border color!
